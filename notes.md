Gemini: 
    Track money growing.

    A dashboard where you enter your stock/crypto holdings. It fetches real-time prices and calculates your total net worth and daily performance.

    Data Points: Ticker symbol (e.g., AAPL, BTC), quantity, purchase price, date bought.

    Visualizations:

    Line Chart: Total portfolio value over time vs. S&P 500.

    Pie Chart: Asset allocation (e.g., 60% Tech, 20% Crypto, 20% Cash).

    Libraries: yfinance (Real-time data), pandas, Plotly (Interactive graphs), Streamlit (for the UI).
